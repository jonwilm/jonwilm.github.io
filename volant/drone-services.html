    AERIAL MAPPING

    • GEOTAGGED AERIAL PHOTOGRAPHY

      • Images have deﬁnitions that go from 1”/pixel to 10”/pixel.

      • Processed to be ﬁxed with the desired reference system.

      • Ideal to measure areas and locate projects with updated photos on programs such as Google Earth.

    • ORTHORECTIFIED AERIAL PHOTOGRAPHY

      • An aerial image that works as a map.

      • All elements that cause error in the geometry are corrected.

      • Used to make very accurate measurements in the horizontal plane.

    • DEM’S AND CONTOUR LINES

      • 3D models that provide the elevation of each visible point in a terrain.

      • Used to calculate volume of land, contour lines, surface areas, and many other applications.

      • Files can be read by GIS software.

      • Contour lines are delivered in DWG to be read in AutoCAD or Microstation.

      • Our methods take 1/5 of the time of traditional terrestrial surveying.

    • DTM’S (DIGITAL TERRAIN MODELS)

      • Capable of delivering bare earth data.

      • DTM’S can remove objects such as trees and/or buildings.

    • OUR ACCURACY AND CAPABILITIES GUARANTEE:

      • No limit on the types and size of terrains that can be mapped.

      • 5 cm accuracy in horizontal and vertical planes.

      • Faster delivery time than all other modern methods.
